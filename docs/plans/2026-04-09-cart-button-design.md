# Cart Button Design

**Goal:** Refresh the user-side `Add to Cart` buttons with a richer dark green visual treatment.

## Direction

Use a shared deep green button language across the customer menu and dish detail screens. Keep the existing shape and layout, but replace the charcoal fill with a darker food-friendly green that feels more premium and slightly more alive.

## Visual Notes

- Base tone: deep green
- Text/icon: white
- Depth: subtle border/shadow
- Scope:
  - `crown-oven-frontend/src/screens/dishes/DishDetailScreen.js`
  - `crown-oven-frontend/src/screens/dishes/MenuScreen.js`
